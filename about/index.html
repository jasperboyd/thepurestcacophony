Beginnings

The Purest Cacophony is the solo project of Jasper Boyd. After playing bass in a number of bands through high school, he was ready to make the transition from accompanist to writer.

I Will Not Fall

In the fall of 2012 Jasper finished writing the collection of songs that became the I Will Not Fall EP and recorded the release both at his apartment in Worcester, MA. & his parent's house in Brunswick, ME.

While in Maine, Jasper enlisted the help of Theo Van Dyck on trumpet. Fast-forward a month and Jonathan Dana was overdubbing the drums at Estabrook Hall, Clark University.

After mixing the tracks himself, Jasper sent them to Mike Tierney to be mastered. The final step was Paul Puiia supplying the album artwork, a beautiful 40-minute exposure of the Maine coast.

Future

During the recording process of I Will Not Fall Jasper grew tired of laborious process of multi-tracking. In an effort to create more natural sounding music Jasper is recording the full-length album completely live.

Look for it here in the Fall.
